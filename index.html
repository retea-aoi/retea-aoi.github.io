ABOUT

Hi, My name is Yongjuan He or you can call me Yongjun. I am currently a junior studnet in the School of Microelectronics Science and Technology, Sun Yat-sen University, P.R. China. My research interests fall upon the Analog/RFIC design, especially focus on the modeling and application of the Passive Mixer.

My aspiration is to contribute to the development of innovative circuits and systems that can revolutionize the way we interact with the world. For example, I am particularly interested in applying my expertise in analog/mixed-signal/RF/mm-wave integrated circuit design to create cutting-edge technologies that can improve the performance and capabilities of wireless communication systems. By developing more efficient and reliable communication systems, we can enhance the way people interact with each other and with the world around them, enabling more seamless and effective communication across different platforms and devices.
